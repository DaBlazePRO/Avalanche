The Batman Movie Review

April 25, 2022

The Batman is now on HBO max which means many people have now seen it. :)

The Batman was a movie that wasn’t like other DCEU movies. It was a lot more dark and dove into the past of Bruce Wayne. While we didn’t get to see much of him on screen, a lot of his actions through out the move were based on his past. The vibe of the movie was very enjoyable where most of the time in gotham it was either raining or very dark. It set the tone of the movie as this dark murder mystery. The villain had a different aproach as we haven’t seen the riddler in the big screen before. It was interesting seeing batman react to the riddlers moves and thinking he is working with the Batman. All things aside this movie is a solid 9 out of 10 stars. I just wish we saw a bit more of Bruce Wayne rather than the Batman for most of the movie. We can never forget EMO BRUCE :) üôè
