Spiderman No Way Home Review

April 25, 2022

It’s been a few months since the webslingers third movie has made its debut, lets review it!

Cameos

There were 4 cameos in the movie. The most wellknown being Andrew Garfields spiderman and Tobey Maguires Spiderman. (OOPS if you didn’t know about it also cry about it). Another one being Matt Murdock from the Netflix/MCU TV Show Daredevil. Matt didn’t play that big of a part, they really could have swapped him for any lawyer but it was a cool cameo for the people that watched and or enjoyed daredevil. Andrew and Tobey on the otherhand helped Tom’s spiderman get through a tough time and gave him hope after the death of Aunt May. Lastly it was Venom from the sony verse which didn’t play a big part either. He was just there and did nothing.

Over all thoughts

The movie was an interesting one with a lot of twists and turns. There were a lot of boring emotional parts which felt unnecessary to the movies plot. I would give the movie a 8 out of 10 because of that. It was really cooling seeing all the villains and the movie did an okay job of explaining them for people that haven’t watched the movie. For the people who complain that you need to have watched all the other Spidermen movies to understand the hype around this movie, cry about it.
